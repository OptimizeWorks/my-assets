Who is eligible for your services?

We support Medicaid-eligible children and teens in New Jersey who are referred through the Children’s System of Care (CSOC). Our team partners with families, care managers, and community organizations to make sure every child receives the care they deserve.

What services do you provide?

Our main services are Behavioral Assistance (BA) and Intensive In-Community (IIC) therapy. BA offers one-on-one support to help with behavioral challenges, while IIC provides therapy and counseling tailored to each child’s needs—all delivered in your home or community.

How do you keep families safe and ensure quality?

Every provider completes background checks, credentialing, and ongoing training. We follow strict documentation and privacy standards, including HIPAA and Medicaid guidelines, so your family’s information stays protected.

How can I start or get more information?

If you’re interested in services or have questions, reach out to your care manager or connect with us directly. We’ll walk you through the process and help you find the right support for your family.
